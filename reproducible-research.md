:::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::: questions 

- What is reproducible research and why should you care?
- What are some common barriers to reproducible research?

::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::

::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::: objectives

- Define reproducible research in practical terms.
- Explain why reproducibility is critical for credibility, reuse, and collaboration.
- Identify common barriers (time, skills, tooling, culture).
- Recognise which barriers are most limiting in your own work and community.
